    Weighted category average

    category_score = earned_points / total_points * 100
    category_contribution = category_score * category_weight

    Current non-final contribution

    current_nonfinal_contribution = current_grade_on_completed_work * nonfinal_weight_portion

    Final exam required

    required_final = (target_course_grade - current_nonfinal_contribution) / final_exam_weight

    Dropped assignment

    When enabled, the lowest completed percentage in that category is removed before the category average is calculated.
